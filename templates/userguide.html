InstaReportAI User Guide

Learn how to use InstaReportAI efficiently to generate professional reports seamlessly.

Introduction to InstaReportAI

Welcome to InstaReportAI, your all-in-one solution for creating professional reports with the help of artificial intelligence. This guide will walk you through all the features and functionalities available on the platform to help you get the most out of InstaReportAI.

InstaReportAI allows you to:

  • Upload multiple posters and documents for information extraction
  • Use AI-powered chatbot assistance for additional information and clarification
  • Preview reports before exporting to PDF
  • Edit generated text with inline editing
  • Insert images from the Image Gallery
  • View and manage your created reports

Let's dive into each of these features in detail.

Uploading Documents

The document upload feature allows you to upload multiple posters and event materials for information extraction and processing.

Step 1: Navigate to the Upload Documents Section

From the dashboard, click on the "Upload Documents" card or navigate to the upload section from the main menu.

Step 2: Select Files for Upload

Click on the "Upload Now" button or drag and drop your files into the designated area. You can upload multiple files at once.

Tip: InstaReportAI supports various file formats including PDF, JPG, PNG, and DOCX. For best results, ensure your files are clear and legible.
Step 3: Process the Uploaded Documents

After uploading, click on "Process Documents" to start the AI-powered information extraction. The system will analyze your documents and extract relevant information about the events.

The document upload interface showing the drag and drop area and file selection button

Note: The processing time depends on the number and size of the uploaded documents. Larger files may take longer to process.

Using Chatbot Assistance

InstaReportAI's intelligent chatbot is designed to help you gather additional information about the event and identify missing details from your uploaded documents.

Step 1: Access the Chatbot

After document processing is complete, you'll see the chatbot interface on the right side of the screen. You can also access it by clicking the chat icon in the bottom right corner.

Step 2: Ask for Additional Information

Use the chatbot to ask questions about the event or to provide details that weren't included in the uploaded documents. For example:

  • "What details are missing from my event poster?"
  • "Can you suggest a better description for this event?"
  • "Help me organize the schedule information."
Step 3: Review and Add Suggestions

The chatbot will provide suggestions and identify missing information. You can add these suggestions directly to your report by clicking "Add to Report" next to each suggestion.

The chatbot interface showing a conversation about event details

Tip: The more specific your questions, the more helpful the chatbot's responses will be. Try to ask one question at a time for best results.

Previewing and Editing Reports

Before finalizing your report, you can preview how it will look and make any necessary edits to the content.

Step 1: Preview Your Report

After the information extraction and any additional input from the chatbot, click on "Preview Report" to see how your final report will look.

Step 2: Use Inline Editing

InstaReportAI features an inline editing tool that allows you to modify any text directly in the preview. Simply click on any text element to edit it.

  • Click on a heading or paragraph to edit the text
  • Use the formatting toolbar to change text styling
  • Rearrange sections by dragging and dropping
Step 3: Save Your Edits

After making changes, click "Save Changes" to update your report. You can continue editing until you're satisfied with the result.

The report preview interface with inline editing capabilities

Important: Always save your changes before navigating away from the preview page to avoid losing your edits.

Using the Image Gallery

The Image Gallery allows you to view, manage, and insert images into your reports.

Step 1: Access the Image Gallery

Click on the "Image Gallery" card on the dashboard or navigate to it from the main menu.

Step 2: Browse and Select Images

Browse through the available images extracted from your uploaded documents. You can also upload additional images by clicking "Upload New Image".

Step 3: Insert Images into Reports

While editing your report, click on the "Insert Image" button to open the Image Gallery. Select an image and click "Insert" to add it to your report at the cursor position.

Step 4: Customize Image Display

After inserting an image, you can:

  • Resize the image by dragging the corner handles
  • Add a caption by clicking below the image
  • Align the image (left, center, right) using the formatting toolbar

The Image Gallery interface showing extracted and uploaded images

Tip: For a professional-looking report, use high-resolution images and add descriptive captions to provide context.

Viewing and Managing Reports

The View Reports section lets you access, manage, and export your generated reports.

Step 1: Access Your Reports

Click on the "View Reports" card on the dashboard or navigate to "My Reports" from the main menu.

Step 2: Browse and Filter Reports

Browse through your previously generated reports. Use the search bar and filters to find specific reports by date, title, or content.

Step 3: View and Edit Reports

Click on a report to view it. If needed, you can make additional edits by clicking the "Edit" button.

Step 4: Export Reports to PDF

When you're satisfied with your report, click the "Export to PDF" button to download a PDF version of your report that you can share or print.

The Reports Management interface showing a list of generated reports

Note: Reports are automatically saved, but you should export important reports to PDF for external sharing and backup purposes.
